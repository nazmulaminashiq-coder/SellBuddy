Return & Refund Policy

30-day money-back guarantee on all products

Home / Return Policy

Our Guarantee

We stand behind every product we sell. If you're not 100% satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Within 30 days of the delivery date
  • In the same condition you received it
  • In the original packaging (if applicable)

How to Request a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email support@sellbuddy.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. Provide Photos: If the item is damaged or defective, please include photos
  3. Wait for Approval: We'll respond within 24 hours with return instructions
  4. Ship the Item: Send the item back to the provided address
  5. Receive Refund: Once we receive the item, we'll process your refund within 48 hours

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately at support@sellbuddy.com with:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damaged/defective item
  • Photos of the packaging

We will provide a full refund including shipping costs or send a replacement at no additional charge.

Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for the correct item to be shipped to you and provide a prepaid return label for the incorrect item.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items that have been used, altered, or damaged by the customer
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Personalized or custom-made items

Return Shipping

Defective/Damaged Items: We cover return shipping costs

Change of Mind: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance. We cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

Exchanges

We don't currently offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received a refund within 10 business days:

  1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again
  2. Contact your credit card company or bank - there may be a processing delay
  3. If you've done both and still haven't received your refund, please contact us at support@sellbuddy.com

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@sellbuddy.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
