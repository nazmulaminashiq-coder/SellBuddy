Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about our shipping and delivery

Home / Shipping Policy

Free Shipping

We offer FREE standard shipping on all orders over $50 worldwide! For orders under $50, a flat rate of $4.99 applies.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Once your order is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

Please note that orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on your location:

Region Estimated Delivery
United States 7-12 business days
Canada 10-15 business days
United Kingdom 10-15 business days
Europe 12-18 business days
Australia / New Zealand 12-20 business days
Rest of World 15-25 business days

Note: These are estimated delivery times. Actual delivery may vary due to customs processing, holidays, or carrier delays.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also contact our support team at any time for tracking updates.

Tracking information may take 24-48 hours to update after your order ships.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in our shipping costs.

We recommend checking with your local customs office for more information about potential charges.

Lost or Delayed Packages

If your package hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery window, please contact us at support@sellbuddy.com. We will work with the carrier to locate your package and resolve the issue.

For packages confirmed as lost by the carrier, we will provide a full refund or reship your order at no additional cost.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is correct and complete when placing your order. We are not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses due to customer error.

If you notice an error in your shipping address, please contact us immediately at support@sellbuddy.com.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our shipping policy, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Email: support@sellbuddy.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
