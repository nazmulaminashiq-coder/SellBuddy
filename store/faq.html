Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location:

  • United States: 7-12 business days
  • Canada & UK: 10-15 business days
  • Europe: 12-18 business days
  • Australia: 12-20 business days
  • Rest of World: 15-25 business days

Please note that these are estimates and actual delivery times may vary due to customs processing.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer FREE shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping rate of $4.99 applies.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships (usually within 1-2 business days), you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also contact our support team with your order number for tracking updates.

Orders & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • PayPal (recommended)
  • Visa
  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

We process orders quickly! If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact us immediately at support@sellbuddy.com with "URGENT" in the subject line.

Cancellation requests made within 2 hours of ordering are usually successful. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled but can be returned for a full refund.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your data. We never store your credit card information on our servers. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways (PayPal/Stripe).

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied for any reason, simply contact us within 30 days of receiving your order.

For defective or damaged items, we'll provide a full refund including shipping costs. For change-of-mind returns, customers are responsible for return shipping.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund:

  1. Email support@sellbuddy.com with your order number
  2. Include reason for return and photos if the item is damaged
  3. We'll respond within 24 hours with return instructions
  4. Refunds are processed within 48 hours of approval

Refunds typically appear in your account within 5-10 business days.

What if my item arrives damaged?

We're sorry if your item arrived damaged! Please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order with:

  • Your order number
  • Photos of the damaged item
  • Photos of the packaging

We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund immediately.
